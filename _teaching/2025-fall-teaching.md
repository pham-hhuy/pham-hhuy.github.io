I began teaching STAT 3011 as a TA since Fall of 2025. Even though STAT 3011 is an introductory course, there's quite a plentiful amount of topics that are central to even more advanced statistics classes. This means a golden opportunity was given to me to grasp a strong understanding of these fundamental concepts in statistics, aiding my career in Data Science.

As though the syllabus may change from semester to semester, here are some of the notable topics we aim to cover in this course:

- Introduction to Inferential Statistics
    - Identifying populations, random samples, etc.
- Introduction to Probability
    - Basics of set theory in probability
    - Common probability rules
- Introduction to Combinatorics (permutations, combinations, etc.)
